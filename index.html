        About the App

        StreamBox is a free, open-source live TV player that aggregates thousands of publicly available IPTV streams from around the world. No subscriptions, no logins, no ads — just live television, anywhere, any time.

        Channel data is sourced from the iptv-org open database. StreamBox does not host any streams itself.

        How to Use StreamBox

        Everything you need to know to get started

        Getting Started

        1
        Pick a Country

        Use the Country dropdown in the sidebar to filter channels by country, or leave it on All Countries to browse everything.

        2
        Choose a Category

        Tap a category tab — General, News, Entertainment, Sports, Kids — to narrow down the channel list.

        3
        Select a Channel

        Tap any channel in the list to start streaming. The player will load and begin playing automatically.

        4
        Unmute if Needed

        If the stream starts muted (e.g. on page reload), tap the 🔇 Tap to unmute banner over the player, or use the mute button in the controls.

        Search

        Use the search bar at the top to find any channel by name or country. Results appear instantly as you type — click or tap a result to play it directly.

        On mobile, tap the Search tab at the bottom to open the channel list with the search bar focused.

        Favourites

        Tap the ♡ heart icon on any channel row to save it. Access all your saved channels instantly from the Favs tab in the sidebar.

        Favourites are saved to your browser and persist across visits.

        Troubleshooting

        Channel won't play? Some streams may be offline or geo-restricted. Tap Try Again or pick a different channel.

        Buffering? This usually means the stream source is slow or your connection is unstable. Try another channel and come back later.

        No sound? Tap the mute button or the Tap to unmute banner over the player.
