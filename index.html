        About the App

        StreamBox is a free, open-source live TV player that aggregates thousands of publicly available IPTV streams from around the world. No subscriptions, no logins, no ads — just live television, anywhere, any time.

        Channel data is sourced from the iptv-org open database. StreamBox does not host any streams itself.
